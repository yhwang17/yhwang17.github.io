Arnav Brahmasandra

Pre-Doc Researcher

University of Chicago

Arnav Brahmasandra is a Pre-Doctoral researcher interested in how machine learning and physical theory can be combined to better understand complex molecular systems. His work focuses on physics-grounded AI for molecular simulation, emphasizing reproducibility and scientific software design. He currently also works as a Software Engineer at Narada AI. Outside of research, he enjoys playing tennis, hiking, occasionally playing the piano, and exploring new coffee shops.
